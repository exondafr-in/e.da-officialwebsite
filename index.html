The human project is driven by the notion of 'group', it is a photographic project that questions the place of an idividu in a whole.

The project revolves around subjects photographed in black on a white background, bare hands and feet, without accessories. the contrast between the individual and his environment is then powerful and leads to observe distinctly the physical characteristics of each person.

In an almost intimate brutality revealing our body and inexpressive soul what remains of our identity?

Who are we ?
